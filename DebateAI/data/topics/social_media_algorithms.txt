Social media algorithms prioritize and recommend content to users based on engagement and behavioral data.

Potential benefits include personalized content delivery and improved user experience.

Concerns include amplification of misinformation, polarization, and addictive usage patterns.

Algorithmic incentives may prioritize engagement over accuracy or social well-being.

Users often lack transparency into how content is ranked or filtered.

Moderation policies interact with algorithms in complex and sometimes unintended ways.

Regulatory scrutiny focuses on transparency, accountability, and societal impact.
